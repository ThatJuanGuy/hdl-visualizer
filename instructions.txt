To run this navigate to the hdl-visualizer directory. Make sure all the
requirements are installed correctly and then type 'flask run' in the terminal.
This should give you a localhost link that you can click or paste into browser to access
the site. To test if it's working, try Mux, DMux, or any primitive chip from project 1.

Some requirements in requirements.txt are probably uncessary and are a by product of when I was
trying to figure stuff out. I just haven't gotten around to removing uncessary ones yet.
